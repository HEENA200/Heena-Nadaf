Tesla Model 3

Overview

Manufacturer: Tesla, Inc.

Production: 2017 – Present

Model years: 2017 – Present

Assembly: Fremont, California, USA; Shanghai, China

Starting Price: Approximately €40,000

Body and Chassis

Class: Compact executive car

Body style: 4-door sedan

Layout: Rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive

Powertrain

Battery: Lithium-ion battery pack

Power output: Up to 450 hp (varies by variant)

Transmission: Single-speed automatic

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,875 mm (113.2 in)

Length: 4,694 mm (184.8 in)

Width: 1,849 mm (72.8 in)

Height: 1,443 mm (56.8 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,610 kg (3,552 lb)

Performance

0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): 3.3 seconds (Performance variant)

Top speed: 261 km/h (162 mph)

Range: Up to 614 km (382 miles) on a full charge (Long Range variant)

Features

Includes Autopilot, over-the-air software updates, touchscreen interface, and premium interior options.
