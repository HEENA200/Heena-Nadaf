Ford Mustang

Overview

Manufacturer: Ford

Production: 1964 – Present

Model years: 1964 – Present

Assembly: Various locations, primarily in the U.S.

Designer: John Najjar and team

Starting Price: Approximately $27,000 (varies by model and condition)

Body and Chassis

Class: Pony car / Sports car

Body style: 2-door coupe / 2-door convertible

Layout: Front-engine, rear-wheel drive

Powertrain

Engine options: 2.3 L I4 (EcoBoost), 5.0 L V8 (GT), among others

Power output: Ranging from 310 hp (EcoBoost) to 450 hp (GT)

Transmission options: 6-speed manual or 10-speed automatic

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,720 mm (107.1 in)

Length: Approximately 4,788 mm (188.5 in)

Width: 1,915 mm (75.4 in)

Height: Approximately 1,383 mm (54.4 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,500 kg (3,307 lb)

Chronology

Predecessor: None (first of its kind)

Successor: None (ongoing production)
