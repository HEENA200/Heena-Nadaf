Mercedes-AMG M-Class

Overview

Manufacturer: Mercedes-Benz (AMG division)

Production: 1997 – Present (evolved into GLE-Class)

Model years: 1998 – Present

Assembly: Alabama, USA (Primary), Graz, Austria (Early Models)

Starting Price: Approximately €100,000 for AMG models

Body and Chassis

Class: Mid-size luxury SUV

Body style: 5-door SUV

Layout: Front-engine, all-wheel drive (4MATIC)

Powertrain

Engine options: 4.0 L twin-turbocharged V8 (AMG variants)

Power output: 603 hp (Mercedes-AMG GLE 63 S)

Transmission: 9-speed automatic (9G-Tronic)

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,995 mm (117.9 in)

Length: 4,924 mm (193.8 in)

Width: 2,018 mm (79.4 in)

Height: 1,772 mm (69.7 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 2,350 kg (5,180 lb)

Performance

0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): 3.7 seconds (AMG GLE 63 S)

Top speed: Limited to 250 km/h (155 mph), can reach 280 km/h (174 mph) with AMG Driver’s Package

Features: Air suspension, active roll stabilization, AMG performance exhaust
