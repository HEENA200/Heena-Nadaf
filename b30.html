Ferrari V12

Overview

Manufacturer: Ferrari

Production: Various models produced from 1947 to present

Notable Models: Ferrari 250 GTO, Ferrari F12 Berlinetta, Ferrari 812 Superfast

Assembly: Maranello, Italy

Body and Chassis

Class: Supercar / Sports Car

Body style: Varies by model – coupe, convertible

Layout: Front-engine or mid-engine, rear-wheel drive

Powertrain

Engine options: 6.0 L to 6.5 L naturally aspirated V12 engines

Power output: Ranges from 600 hp to 800 hp, depending on the model

Transmission: 7-speed dual-clutch automatic (varies by model)

Dimensions

Dimensions vary significantly depending on the model.

Example (Ferrari 812 Superfast):

Wheelbase: 2,670 mm (104.3 in)

Length: 4,657 mm (183.3 in)

Width: 1,971 mm (77.6 in)

Height: 1,276 mm (50.2 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,525 kg (3,362 lb)

Performance

0–100 km/h (0–62 mph): 2.9 seconds (for 812 Superfast)

Top speed: 340 km/h (211 mph) (for 812 Superfast)

Features: Iconic sound, advanced aerodynamics, and cutting-edge technology
