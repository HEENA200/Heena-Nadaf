Aventador

Overview

Manufacturer: Lamborghini

Production: 1990 – 2001

Model years: 1990 – 2001

Assembly: Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy

Designer: Marcello Gandini

Starting Price: Approximately $200,000

Body and Chassis

Class: Supercar

Body style: 2-door coupe / 2-door roadster

Layout: Mid-engine, all-wheel drive

Powertrain

Engine: 5.7 L V12 (early models), 6.0 L V12 (later models)

Power output: 492 PS (485 hp) (early models), up to 580 PS (572 hp) (Diablo VT 6.0)

Transmission: 5-speed manual / 6-speed manual (Diablo VT 6.0)

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,560 mm (100.8 in)

Length: 4,430 mm (174.4 in)

Width: 1,900 mm (74.8 in)

Height: 1,165 mm (45.9 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,650 kg (3,638 lb)

Chronology

Predecessor: Lamborghini Countach

Successor: Lamborghini Murciélago
