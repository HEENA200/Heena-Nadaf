Countach

Overview

Manufacturer: Lamborghini

Production: 1974 – 1990

Model years: 1974 – 1990

Assembly: Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy

Designer: Marcello Gandini

Starting Price: Approximately $120,000 (varies by model and condition)

Body and Chassis

Class: Supercar

Body style: 2-door coupe

Layout: Mid-engine, rear-wheel drive

Powertrain

Engine: 4.0 L V12 (early models), 5.2 L V12 (later models)

Power output: 375 PS (370 hp) (early models), up to 455 PS (450 hp) (Countach 25th Anniversary)

Transmission: 5-speed manual

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,430 mm (95.7 in)

Length: 4,400 mm (173.2 in)

Width: 1,990 mm (78.3 in)

Height: 1,125 mm (44.4 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,600 kg (3,527 lb)

Chronology

Predecessor: Lamborghini Miura

Successor: Lamborghini Diablo
