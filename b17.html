Gallardo

Overview

Manufacturer: Lamborghini

Production: 2003 – 2013

Model years: 2003 – 2013

Assembly: Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy

Designer: Luc Donckerwolke

Starting Price: Approximately $200,000 (varies by model and condition)

Body and Chassis

Class: Supercar

Body style: 2-door coupe / 2-door spyder

Layout: Mid-engine, all-wheel drive

Powertrain

Engine: 5.0 L V10 (early models), 5.2 L V10 (later models)

Power output: 500 PS (493 hp) (early models), up to 570 PS (562 hp) (Gallardo LP 570-4)

Transmission: 6-speed manual or 6-speed automated manual (E-Gear)

Dimensions

Wheelbase: 2,460 mm (96.9 in)

Length: 4,250 mm (167.3 in)

Width: 1,900 mm (74.8 in)

Height: 1,165 mm (45.9 in)

Curb weight: Approximately 1,520 kg (3,351 lb)

Chronology

Predecessor: None (first V10 model)

Successor: Lamborghini Huracán
